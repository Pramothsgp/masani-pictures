About Masani Pictures

From Madurai backdrops to Chennai skylines, Masani Pictures develops and produces Kollywood stories with heart, swagger, and craft. As a premier Tamil film production company, we've been creating blockbuster Tamil movies for over 20 years. Our diverse portfolio includes auteur dramas, mass entertainers, action thrillers, and bold original content that showcases South Indian cinema to global audiences.
